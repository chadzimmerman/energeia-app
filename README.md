Energe.ia
Energe.ia is a gamified habit-tracking application that transforms personal growth into a medieval, retro-styled RPG adventure. Heavily influenced by Eastern Palamite mysticism and medieval aesthetics, the app serves as a "cozy" digital sanctuary for those seeking to cultivate virtue and excise vice.

In this system, your character is a direct reflection of your real-world choices. The core mechanic revolves around the concept of Energeia‚Äîthe actualization of potential.

Virtue & Vice: Gain Energeia by completing positive habits or successfully rejecting negative ones. Conversely, falling back into bad habits causes your character to lose HP.

RPG Progression: Start by choosing from 3 unique starter classes. As you develop your real-life discipline, your character grows in power, unlocking the ability to purchase armor, weapons, and items from the Market.

Aesthetic: A retro, "low-fi" cozy vibe that encourages a peaceful but disciplined state of mind.
